Venue Management

We are able to represent your venue as 'Venue Coordinators ' in which we create and negotiate contracts, coordinate, and decorate all proposed events held at the agreed upon venue.

VENUES AND VENUE EVENT MANAGEMENT



VENUE COORDINATION

The Royal Carpet Events will ensure the following:

  • Contract terms are adhered to
  • Client special requests are handled
  • Keeping staff (sometimes includes catering/bar) on task
  • Managing the venue to the expectations of the owner and acting as an advocate for the venue and it's staff
  • Overseeing event closure (ie. making sure guests safely depart, load out is on time with vendors according to owners requests, etc.)
  • Running tasks during the event
  • We also coordinate with the Gulfarium Marine Adventure Park. You can visit their website www.gulfarium.com and go to Wedding & Private Parties. For information & booking just call or fill out the event planner form.
